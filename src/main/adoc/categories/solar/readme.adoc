== Example

Computing example.

* Look at solar map showing your kWh/kWc per year
** e.g. 1000-1100 kWh/kWc
* Solar panel gives 370 Wc and you have 12 which makes 370 x 12 = 4440 Wc
* With ratio, you can produce between 4440 kWh and 4884 kWh
